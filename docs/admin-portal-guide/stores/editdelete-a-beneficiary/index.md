# Edit/Delete a Beneficiary

## What this guide covers

Updates or removes an existing beneficiary record.

## Steps

**Step 1:** Start by going to the Stores screen by clicking here.

**Step 2:** Search for the beneficiary by name.
**Step 3:** Click on the Beneficiaries tab.

**Step 4:** Once you find the beneficiary you are looking for, click on the stacked dots to open the option window.

![Step 2](./img/step-2.png)

Step 5 to Edit: Click on Edit to open the selected beneficiaries drawer to edit. Continue on to Step 6.

Step 5 to Delete: Click on Delete to reveal this modal for you to take the next step in deleting the beneficiary.

**Step 6:** Edit each area as needed making sure to fill in each “*”required field.

![Step 3](./img/step-3.png)

## Notes

:::note
If you need to stop your creation click here. Please be aware that your info will not be saved.
:::

# Add an Image to a Product

## What this guide covers

Guides operators through uploading and assigning a display image to a product so customers see accurate visuals when ordering through digital channels.

## Steps

**Step 1:** Start by going to the Products screen by clicking here.

![Step 1](./img/step-1.png)

**Step 2:** Click the 3 dots to reveal a panel. Click Copy.

![Step 2](./img/step-2.png)

**Step 3:** Fill in each field as needed.

**Step 4:** When you are done click Save.

![Step 3](./img/step-3.png)

## Notes

:::note
You can search Products by entering the Name or Code.
:::

:::note
Depending on your screen size you may need to scroll to see the other columns.
:::

:::note
There are other options in the window  but for this step we are just looking at Images. Others are discussed else where. Please go to the Table of Contents to find where.
:::

:::note
If you need to stop click Cancel. Please be aware that your info will not be saved.
:::

:::note
If you need this image to be the Primary image shown, click to toggle to Yes.
:::

:::note
If you need to add another image, click here and follow the same steps as in Step 3.
:::

# Edit a Slot

## What this guide covers

Updates an existing slot's configuration, such as its label or linked items.

## Steps

**Step 1:** Start by going to the Products screen by clicking here.

**Step 2:** Click the Slots tab.
**Step 3:** You can search Slots by entering the Names or Codes or by Tags.

**Step 4:** Click the 3 dots to reveal a panel. Click Edit.

![Step 2](./img/step-2.png)

**Step 5:** Click on either of the blue links to go to that area and edit.

**Step 6:** Edit the areas as needed making sure to fill in each “*”required field and any other valuable information.

![Step 3](./img/step-3.png)

## Notes

:::note
There are other options in the window  but for this step we are just looking at Edit. Others are discussed else where. Please go to the Table of Contents to find where.

# Restaurant Profile Setup

Your restaurant profile is the foundation of your presence on the Atlas Platform. It controls how your restaurant appears to customers in the app and website — and feeds into delivery zone calculations, order routing, and reporting.

## How to Access

**Admin Portal → Restaurants → [Your Restaurant Name] → Profile**

## What You Can Edit

| Field | Description | Reflects in app? |
|---|---|---|
| Restaurant name | Display name shown to customers | ✅ Within 15 mins |
| Address | Full street address — used for map and delivery radius | ✅ Within 15 mins |
| Phone number | Shown on order confirmation page | ✅ Within 15 mins |
| Operating hours | Open/close per day, per channel | ✅ Immediately |
| Public holiday hours | Date-specific overrides | ✅ Automatically on the day |
| Temporarily closed | Closes all channels without deleting hours | ✅ Immediately |

## Setting Public Holiday Hours

1. Go to **Profile → Public Holiday Hours**
2. Click **Add Holiday**
3. Select the date from the calendar
4. Choose: **Fully Closed** or set reduced hours
5. Save — the override applies automatically on that date and reverts the following day

## Temporarily Closing Your Restaurant

Use the **Temporarily Closed** toggle when you need to stop taking orders without changing your standard hours — for example, during an unexpected staffing issue or equipment failure.

:::warning
When toggled on, **all channels are closed immediately** — Dine-in, Delivery, and Takeaway. Customers will see your restaurant as closed in the app. Remember to toggle it off when you reopen.
:::

## What You Cannot Edit

| Field | Why | How to change |
|---|---|---|
| GPS Coordinates | Locked to prevent delivery zone errors | Contact your regional office |
| Restaurant ID | System-generated, immutable | N/A |
| Market assignment | Set during onboarding | Contact your BMU |

## FAQs

**How quickly do profile changes appear in the customer app?**
Most changes (name, address, phone) appear within 15 minutes. Operating hours and closure status apply immediately.

**Can I manage multiple restaurants from one login?**
Yes — if your account has been granted access to multiple restaurants, you'll see a restaurant selector at the top of the portal. Switch between locations without logging out.

**Who can see the changes I make?**
Your regional office can view all changes in the audit log — every edit is logged with a timestamp and your username.

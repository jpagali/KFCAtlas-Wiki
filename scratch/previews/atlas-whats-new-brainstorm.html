Passwordless Authentication

Removes password dependency and enables OTP login for faster, more secure customer authentication, especially in high-friction mobile flows.

Expected impact Higher login conversion and reduced password reset friction for customers returning to order.
Where it fits Markets focused on conversion, repeat ordering, and simplifying authentication support overhead.
Read Product Guide
Business problem Passwords create login abandonment and customer support friction during checkout and re-engagement.
How to use it Enable OTP-based authentication and configure the login experience for supported flows.
Best-fit markets Teams prioritizing reduced friction in sign-in and better mobile conversion performance.

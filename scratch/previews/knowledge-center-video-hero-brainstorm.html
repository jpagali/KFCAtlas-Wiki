Direction

Cinematic full bleed

The strongest brand move. This is the version that makes the homepage feel less like internal docs and more like a product surface with KFC energy.

  • Best if we want immediate visual impact.
  • Needs the most discipline on contrast and compression.
  • Works best with slower, close-cropped footage.
Direction

Split focus

The safest middle ground. The content feels stable on the left while the right side becomes a controlled stage for motion.

  • Best balance of readability and storytelling.
  • Makes the search box feel less visually vulnerable.
  • Easiest version to defend in review.
Direction

Subtle motion window

Keeps the current hero mostly intact, but adds a premium moving accent. Useful if we want motion without redesigning the whole page.

  • Lowest implementation and performance risk.
  • Feels more editorial than immersive.
  • Good fallback if autoplay policy or file weight becomes a concern.
